Common AI Implementation Mistakes to Avoid

Now let me save you from some of the biggest mistakes I've seen people make! These pitfalls can absolutely derail your AI success, and honestly, I've made some of these mistakes myself when I was first starting out. Learning from my failures (and the failures of others) will save you time, money, and a whole lot of frustration!

  • Over-automating: Some tasks still need human touch
  • No training: Teams need to understand how to work with AI
  • Ignoring data quality: AI is only as good as the data it receives

  • Choosing complex solutions: Start simple, then scale
